What is Pulse?

Pulse is the best way to listen to music. Seamlessly switch between local songs and videos to streamed songs.
Whether you want to discover new artists or play your own music, Pulse can do it all.

Pulse is also a platform for music artists. Simply upload your songs on the website,
wait for approval and your song will instantly directly be promoted to listeners. Artists will get in depth feedback on how your songs are doing and much more.
